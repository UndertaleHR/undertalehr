Frequently Asked Questions

This page lists some questions that no one’s asked yet, but perhaps someone will one day.

What is Undertale?

Undertale is a globally famous RPG (role-playing game) released by Toby Fox in 2015. The game is inspired by many older games, mainly ones from Japan, such as the Mother/Earthbound series, Cave Story, Shin Megami Tensei, Touhou Project etc. The game is available in English and Japanese.

What is UndertaleHR?

UndertaleHR (UTHR, Undertale in Croatian) is the Croatian translation of the globally famous RPG (role-playing game) published by Toby Fox in 2015 and inspired by older games of mainly Japanese descent. The game is not officially available in the Croatian language, but the UndertaleHR mod (modification) adds a Croatian translation along with the English and Japanese versions of the game.

Is UTHR the official translation?

It’s not. The game is officially playable only in English or Japanese. The English version is the original one, while the Japanese translation was overseen by Toby Fox. The Croatian translation was made without Fox’s involvement, so it is not official. Still,Toby Fox said he likes seeing unofficial translations, so this shouldn’t be a problem to him 🙂

Why is UndertaleHR?

Since its release in 2015, Undertale has been highly acclaimed by audiences worldwide, but it never gained much popularity in Croatia. As a fan of the game and, as luck would have it, a Croat, I decided to translate the game to Croatian so Croats could experience it too – whether they don’t know English or if they’d like to meet the characters and story in a language that feels closer to them.

Can I play UTHR if I’m not a Croat?

Of course! You shouldn’t have any trouble understanding the Croatian translation if you come from another Balkan country and speak a similar language.
Anyone who understands Croatian is welcome to play this mod!

Is UTHR free?

Yes! You can download and install the Croatian mod free of charge! However, in order to play it, you will first need the original version of the game, which is paid. Undertale can be bought on various platforms (I recommend Steam, but there are also HumbleBundle, GOG, and Microsoft).

Which platforms can I play UTHR on?

The only platform the mod has been tested and confirmed to work on is Windows.
